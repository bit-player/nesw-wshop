NESW has been awarded an Idea Grant from the National Association of Science Writers to organize a one-day workshop called “Telling Science Stories with Code and Data". The idea is to create new and better SCIENCE stories by exploring and leveraging the new tools available to journalists—computer programming and data analysis.
